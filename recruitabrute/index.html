INTRO

Recruit A Brute is crazy

In Recruit A Brute, the player fights there way through different rooms filled with enemies in a turn-based combat system. The goal is to get to final level and defeat the might executioner. As the player progresses through rooms, they're given the option to upgrade their existing brutes, or to recruit new ones!

Combat

Run, attack, dodge, flee, cast spells. You can do it all in Recruit A Brute!

Persistent units

Brutes keep all their stats between combats. Make sure to keep your team of brutes healthy!

Rewards

Upgrade em, heal em or let em die to make room for new ones? It's up to you as you manage your team of brutes!
